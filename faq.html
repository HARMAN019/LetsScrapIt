FAQ'S

Do you accept scrap cars / vehicles in which some parts are missing?

Yes, we do accept such vehicles. Though it will have implications on the price offered to the owner.

What is the process to submit my scrap vehicle / car to Let's Scrap It?

You can schedule pickup by providing us the details of your vehicle after registration. You can also call us on -9319378XXX. Our customer facing process is hassle free and has been designed keeping your convenience in mind.

Do you accept scrap vehicles & cars that have no wheels, to recycle?

Yes, we do collect such vehicles. Anything for recycling old vehicles!

Should my old / scrap vehicle have all parts intact if I wish to engage with Let's Scrap It?

No, but the quote you receive from our website is based on the assumption that your vehicle has all the parts in place. The final rate will be decided after a physical inspection, which maybe on-site or remote. Be assured that Let's Scrap It will give you the best and most transparent cost.

Should the scrap Vehicle be in running condition?

Not necessary! If the vehicle is in running condition, you can drop it at the collection Centre or our executive/s will collect it up from the pick-up address provided. If the vehicle is not in running condition, it will be towed to the collection Centre

Does my scrap car have to be free from damage?

No, Let's Scrap It accepts damaged cars. These will be termed as accident End-of- Life Vehicles. The quote you receive will depend on the extent of damage to the vehicle and its parts

What all documents do I need to submit along with the vehicle?

Along with the keys, the vehicle owner should submit the following documents: vehicle RC book, address proof, PAN Card of the owner and a cancelled cheque (for payment purposes only).

How much will I receive for my scrap Vehicle?

Based on the vehicle's year, make, model, location, condition, etc. we will offer you an approximate price on call/ website. The final offer price will be made by the team post physical inspection of the car. The offer made will be transparent and the best.

Will the RTO authority be informed of my vehicle's scrapping?

Yes, but don't worry we will take care of that for you. After completely dismantling your vehicle, we will send the destruction certificate to the respective RTO authority and notify them.

The registration of my vehicle is not in my name. Will Let's Scrap It accept my vehicle?

In order to prevent fraud, Cero accepts vehicles only from the registered owner. Payment will be made to the registered owner of the vehicle.
